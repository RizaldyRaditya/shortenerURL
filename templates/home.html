Redirection to any page in your site!

With ShortURL, you can also make a smaller URL that will work for any page on your site. Let's say that you have a website with the homepage that is at:

https://siswa.smktelkom-mlg.sch.id/welcome

Entering that URL into TinyURL will create a URL like

http://shorturl.com/1

With this you can then redirect someone to anywhere within your site by appending a slash and the pages filename to it. So if you have a page at https://siswa.smktelkom-mlg.sch.id/welcome , you can use the URL http://shorturl.com/1 and going to this URL will forward the visitor to the page in your website.
